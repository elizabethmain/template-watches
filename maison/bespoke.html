Bespoke

Your vision,
our
craftsmanship

Every Template Watch can be commissioned as a unique piece. From dial colour to case metal to strap leather, our master horologists will work with you over six to eighteen months to create a timepiece that belongs to no one else.

01

The Consultation

We begin with a private appointment at our Geneva atelier or at one of our partner salons. You will meet your assigned master horologist and discuss the brief — from movement type to case dimensions.

02

Design & Sketching

Over 4–8 weeks, our design team produces hand-drawn sketches and wax models. You will review two to three distinct directions before approving the final design.

03

Manufacture

Your movement is assigned to a single master horologist who will assemble every component from first to last, signing the caseback upon completion.

04

Delivery

Your piece arrives in our signature rosewood presentation box, accompanied by a hand-signed certificate, spare strap, and a personal letter from your horologist.
