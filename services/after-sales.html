Services

Lifetime
service & care

Every Template Watch comes with a lifetime service commitment. Whether your piece needs a routine service, a crystal replacement, or a full movement overhaul, our Geneva atelier is here.

We recommend scheduling a full service every 5 to 7 years to maintain the precision and longevity of your movement. During a service, our watchmakers disassemble, clean, lubricate, and reassemble every component using only original Template Watches parts — never third-party substitutes. Genuine leather straps and all external components are inspected and replaced as necessary to restore your timepiece to its original condition.

O

Original Parts Only

Every component used in servicing is sourced directly from our Geneva atelier.

G

Geneva Atelier Work

All service work is performed in-house by our master watchmakers in Geneva.

W

3-Year Service Warranty

Every completed service is covered by a three-year warranty on parts and labour.
