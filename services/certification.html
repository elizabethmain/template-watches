Poinçon de
Genève Certified

Every Template Watch movement carries the Poinçon de Genève — the most rigorous certification standard in Swiss horology, verified by independent inspectors.

The Poinçon de Genève, or Geneva Seal, is awarded only to movements manufactured and cased within the Canton of Geneva that meet a precise set of criteria covering finishing, component quality, and mechanical performance. Each movement is examined by independent inspectors appointed by the Republic and Canton of Geneva — not by our atelier — ensuring complete impartiality. The hallmark is applied physically to the movement itself, a permanent record of the standard achieved at the moment of manufacture.

Every Template Watch is delivered with a full documentation set including the original certificate of authenticity bearing the movement serial number, the Poinçon de Genève hallmark certificate issued by the official body, a chronometer rating certificate where applicable, and a leather-bound record of the watchmaker responsible for its assembly. Should your original documentation be lost, stolen, or required for insurance or estate purposes, we are able to issue replacement certificates upon verification of original ownership.

Please note: verification of original ownership is required. Allow 4–6 weeks.
