Request a
Private Viewing

Our Geneva atelier receives guests by private appointment only. Whether you are considering your first Template Watch or returning to commission a bespoke piece, we will prepare a curated selection for your visit.

Each appointment is arranged personally by our atelier team and tailored entirely to your interests. You will be welcomed into a private salon, where a master horologist will guide you through our current collection and, should you wish, discuss the possibilities of a bespoke commission. Appointments typically last between 60 and 90 minutes, and we ask for at least five working days' notice to ensure full preparation.
