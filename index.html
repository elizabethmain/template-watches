Our Heritage

Born on the
shores of
Lac Léman

In 1887, master watchmaker Édouard Renard established our atelier in the heart of Geneva's Plainpalais district. Drawing on the city's centuries-long tradition of horology, he fused Calvinist precision with a distinctly Genevan artistry — a philosophy that every Template Watch carries to this day.

Our workshops remain on the same cobbled street, now home to twelve master horologists who assemble each movement by hand under 40× magnification, never more than twelve pieces per day.
